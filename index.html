About Syncnovate

Syncnovate was founded by a seasoned post-graduate Data Analytics professor with a vision to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands. Our mission is to equip undergraduate and early-career students (ages 18–25) with practical, job-ready skills in critical business domains.

We believe in quality over quantity, which is why we limit our batch sizes to just 5 students. This ensures truly personalized attention, in-depth learning, and a supportive environment where every student's growth is prioritized.
